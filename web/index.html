Welcome to Town Square!

This is a chat app where you can move around and change your color.

Move by clicking and dragging anywhere (once you close this tooltip, by pressing 'x' in the top right) and change your color by clicking on the rainbow square in the top right of the chat window.

To send a message, click on the text box below (or press enter), type something, and press send (or press enter)!

Hopefully you enjoy this!
